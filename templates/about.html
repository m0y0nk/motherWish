🎯 Our Mission & Vision
What drives us every day to make healthcare better

🎯 Our Mission

To make quality healthcare accessible, affordable, and convenient for every Indian citizen. We bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers through innovative digital solutions.

🔭 Our Vision

To become India's most trusted digital healthcare ecosystem — empowering 1 billion+ people with instant access to doctors, hospitals, emergency care, and health management tools.

📅 Instant Appointments

Book appointments with 20+ verified specialists across 10+ hospitals. Get real-time OPD queue numbers and QR-code based check-in — no more long waiting lines.

🚑 Emergency Response

One-tap access to 108 ambulance services. Live ambulance tracking, blood bank availability, and AI-powered route optimization for the fastest emergency response.

🩺 Doctor Network

A curated network of India's best doctors from Apollo, Fortis, AIIMS, Narayana Health, and more. Filter by specialization, experience, fees, and patient ratings.

🛡️ Insurance Integration

Seamless cashless treatment with major insurers — Star Health, HDFC Ergo, Bajaj Allianz, and government schemes like PM-JAY, CGHS, and ESIC.

💊 Medicine Management

Complete prescription tracking, dosage reminders, multi-language instructions, and pharmacy price comparison. Never miss a dose again with our smart reminder system.

How she became India's most loved AI doctor companion

Dr. Myra — Your 24×7 AI Medical Assistant

Dr. Myra was created from a simple belief: every patient deserves immediate, caring medical guidance regardless of the time of day. Millions of Indians face health anxieties at 2 AM with no one to turn to — Dr. Myra is always there.

Built by a team of doctors, AI engineers, and patient advocates, Dr. Myra can check your symptoms, book doctors, find hospitals, manage emergencies, and respond via text or voice — just like a real doctor assistant.

Today, Dr. Myra helps 50,000+ patients across 8 cities, answering millions of health queries and connecting them with Motherish's trusted network. And she never sleeps! 💙
