⚡ Common Child Emergencies — What To Do
Stay calm. Follow these quick steps while help is on the way.
🌡️

High Fever

Sponge with lukewarm water. Give age-appropriate paracetamol. See a doctor if 103°F+.

🫁

Choking

For infants: 5 back blows + 5 chest thrusts. For older kids: Heimlich maneuver. Call 108 immediately.

💊

Swallowed Something

Don't induce vomiting. Note what was swallowed. Rush to nearest hospital or call poison helpline.

🤕

Head Injury

Keep child still. Apply ice pack. If vomiting or unconscious, call 108 and rush to ER.
