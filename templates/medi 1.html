❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment? +
Go to the Book Appointment section, select your doctor, choose date & time, and confirm. You'll receive a QR code and OPD queue number instantly.
How does blood bank work? +
Our live blood bank shows real-time availability across all partner hospitals. Contact the hospital directly or call our helpline for emergency requests.
Is my data secure? +
Yes! All medical records are encrypted with AES-256 and stored on secure Indian servers compliant with IT Act 2000 and DPDP Act 2023.
Which insurance plans are accepted? +
We support Star Health, HDFC Ergo, Bajaj Allianz, New India Assurance, United India, and 15+ more insurers for cashless treatment.
How do I call an ambulance? +
Click the red 108 button on the home page for free government ambulances, or choose from private hospital ambulances for faster response in premium hospitals.
Can I cancel or reschedule? +
Yes, appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 2 hours before the scheduled time via your patient dashboard or by calling our helpline.

    Emergency Symptoms — Seek Immediate Care

    Severe allergic reaction (rash + swelling + difficulty breathing), chest pain, sudden vision loss, severe vomiting — Call 108 immediately

    ⚠️
    Side Effects to Watch
    • Metformin: Nausea, stomach pain (take with food)
    • Amlodipine: Ankle swelling, dizziness on standing
    • Atorvastatin: Muscle pain or weakness (report to doctor)
    • Azithromycin: Irregular heartbeat (rare — call doctor)
    • Salbutamol: Tremors, rapid heartbeat (expected, mild)
    🔄
    Drug Interaction Warnings
    • ⚠️ Atorvastatin + Azithromycin: increased muscle damage risk
    • ⚠️ Amlodipine + grapefruit juice: increases drug level
    • ⚠️ Metformin + alcohol: increases lactic acidosis risk
    • ⚠️ Azithromycin + antacids: reduces antibiotic absorption
    • ✅ Always tell your doctor ALL medicines you are taking
    🌡️
    Storage Instructions
    • Metformin: Room temperature below 30°C, dry place
    • Amlodipine: Below 25°C, away from light
    • Atorvastatin: Below 30°C, original packaging
    • Salbutamol Inhaler: Below 30°C, avoid puncturing canister
    • Azithromycin: Below 25°C, keep dry
    🍺
    Alcohol Warnings
    • 🔴 Metformin + Alcohol: risk of lactic acidosis
    • 🔴 Amlodipine + Alcohol: severe blood pressure drop
    • 🔴 Azithromycin + Alcohol: liver stress, nausea
    • 🟡 Atorvastatin: limit alcohol (liver concern)
    • ✅ Pantoprazole: occasional alcohol is acceptable
    🧊
    Special Storage (Fridge)
    • Unopened insulin vials: 2°C–8°C refrigerator (never freeze)
    • Some eye drops require refrigeration — check label
    • Suppositories: refrigerate if ambient >25°C
    • Opened insulin: room temp up to 28 days
    • Probiotics: refrigerate to maintain live cultures
    🚨
    Emergency Symptoms
    • Severe allergic reaction: rash + swelling + breathing difficulty
    • Severe chest pain or palpitations after medication
    • Sudden confusion, vision loss, or slurred speech
    • Uncontrolled bleeding after blood thinners
    • → Call 108 immediately for any of the above
