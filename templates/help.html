❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment? ▼
Go to the "Book Appointment" page, select your doctor, choose a date and time slot, and confirm. You'll get an instant OPD queue number and QR code for check-in.
Is the 108 ambulance service free? ▼
Yes! 108 is a free government ambulance service available 24/7 across India. You can also call directly from our Home page.
How do I check blood availability? ▼
The Home page shows real-time blood bank availability with live updates every 5 seconds. For emergency requests, call our helpline at 9343335183.
Which insurance providers are supported? ▼
We support Star Health, HDFC Ergo, Bajaj Allianz, New India Assurance, United India Insurance, and government schemes like PM-JAY, CGHS, and ESIC.
Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment? ▼
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 2 hours before your appointment time. Contact our helpline or use the chat assistant for help.
How does the medicine reminder work? ▼
Our medicine management system tracks all your prescriptions, sends reminders at scheduled times, and even supports multi-language instructions in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam.
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely. We follow HIPAA-grade data protection standards. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your medical data with third parties.
